Project Details

This project involves the development of a Customer Feedback Dashboard tailored for the hospitality industry. It leverages data analysis to identify key areas of improvement, providing businesses with actionable insights to enhance customer satisfaction and operational efficiency. By visualizing customer feedback and trends, the dashboard supports data-driven strategies for improved service delivery, targeted marketing, and better guest retention.

The dashboard incorporates metrics like overall ratings, Net Promoter Scores (NPS), and discovery channels to provide a comprehensive view of guest preferences and experiences. It empowers businesses to adapt dynamically to market trends and customer expectations, fostering loyalty and sustained growth.

Customer Feedback Dashboard

Developed a data-driven dashboard to visualize customer feedback for a hotel business, focusing on areas for improvement, customer satisfaction, and enhanced business performance. Insights include ratings, NPS, demographics, and trends over time to support targeted marketing and service upgrades.

Key Insights

  • Overall Rating: Highlights service improvement opportunities.
  • NPS: Mixed loyalty insights for better customer retention.
  • Visitor Purpose: Dominance of vacationers and business travelers guides tailored services.
  • Gender Distribution: Balanced offerings needed for male and female guests.
  • Rating Categories: Facilities and dining areas need enhancement.
  • Discovery Trends: Focus on effective channels like partnerships and expand social media reach.

Business Benefits

  • Improves customer satisfaction and retention through actionable feedback.
  • Supports targeted marketing and service customization.
  • Tracks trends to adapt business strategies dynamically.
